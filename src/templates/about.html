About WeatherBuddy

WeatherBuddy is a web application that provides up-to-date weather information for any city in the world.

Our goal is to help people plan their day by providing accurate and reliable weather forecasts, along with useful information on clothing recommendations for the current weather conditions.

WeatherBuddy also provides additional location data, including latitude, longitude, population, and timezone, to give users a comprehensive overview of their selected city.
